<h1><strong>Applied Learning Programme (ALP) at Holy Innocents’ High School (HIHS)</strong></h1>
<h4><strong>Journalism and Broadcasting - Empowering Leaders and Innovators</strong></h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Applied Learning Programme (ALP) nurtures students" src="/images/Applied%20Learning%20Programme%20/ALP_1.jpg">
</div>
<p>At Holy Innocents’ High School, we integrate technology and media into
our learning to prepare students for the future.</p>
<p>Our Applied Learning Programme (ALP) nurtures students into innovators
who can advocate and make a difference in their communities.</p>
<p><strong>Key Outcomes of ALP:</strong>
<br>Our ALP aims to produce graduates who are:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Innovators</strong>: Able to understand issues from different
angles and devise creative solutions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Effective Communicators</strong>: Able to articulate ideas clearly
and persuasively using various forms of media.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Leaders</strong>: Motivated to lead with courage and serve with
compassion, embodying the HIHS Good Samaritan Ethos and our core values
of humility, integrity, respect, and love.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Achieving Outcomes Through ALP:</strong>
</p>
<p>Our students reach these outcomes by:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Engaging in both classroom-based and hands-on learning experiences.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participating in assessments that challenge them to apply their knowledge
in real-world contexts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Taking up leadership opportunities within the school, supported by our
dedicated teachers.</p>
</li>
</ul>
