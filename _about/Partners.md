<h3><strong>Partners</strong></h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.facebook.com/HI.psg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Parent Support Group</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://m.facebook.com/hialumni/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Alumni</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong><u>Caritas Singapore</u></strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>HIHS has establised strong partnership with Caritas Singapore since 2021.
Through service learning projects, our students serve the needs of the
beneficiaries from Montfort Care, Good Shepherd Student Care, and Morning
Star Student Care Services, which are member organisations under Caritas
Singapore.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong><u>FamChamps</u>&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Beginning 2025, HIHS partners FamChamps in encouraging Secondary 2 Peer
Support Leaders to participate in the FamChamps Programme. Through activities
like camp and mentoring, these students&nbsp; learn more about family values
and are empowered as young family champions to help advocate for the building
of strong families in the community.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong><u>ECG Partners</u>&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>HIHS works closely with Junior Colleges and Institutes of Higher learning
(e.g., polytechnics and ITEs) to organise ECG talks, visits, learning journeys,
and workshops to help our students explore their different education and
career pathways.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>To provide students with authentic experiences to learn more about the
different industries, HIHS also partners Access SG to organise learning
journeys (for instance, to Ritz Carlton hotel). During these experiences,
students interact with professionals and industry practitioners to understand
more about the world of work.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong><u>STUCK Design</u></strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>STUCK Design serves as HIHSâ€™s key design partner, bringing creative expertise
and strategic imagination to reimagine how learning is designed. STUCK
has worked alongside HIHS educators to apply design thinking principles
to curriculum, assessment, and school culture. Their partnership has helped
teachers prototype lessons, refine feedback practices, and build capacity
for designing learning spaces with empathy.&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong><u>Success Academy</u></strong>&nbsp;
<br>HIHS partners with the SUSS Success Centre to explore learning opportunities
in Shenzhen. Leveraging its extensive industry network and experience in
designing authentic learning experiences, the centre supports HIHS in curating
meaningful overseas learning experiences for students.&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong><u>Mustard Seed Project Place-Based Education Locations</u></strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Singapore Maritime Gallery&nbsp;</p>
<p>National Museum of Singapore&nbsp;</p>
<p>National Gallery Singapore&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
